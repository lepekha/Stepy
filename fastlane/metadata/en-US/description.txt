Stepy turns your daily steps into a beautiful year-long heatmap — like a contribution graph for your walking.

COUNT EVERY STEP
Stepy reads step data straight from Apple Health. No extra hardware, no battery drain. Open the app and today's count is right there.

HIT YOUR DAILY GOAL
Set a step goal and follow a live progress bar. Stepy shows how close you are and celebrates when you reach 100%.

SEE YOUR WHOLE YEAR
Every day is a colored square. The more you walk, the brighter it glows. Tap any day to see its exact step count and spot your streaks at a glance.

STATS THAT MATTER
Daily average, all-time total, and your record day — always one tap away.

HOME SCREEN WIDGETS
Add small or medium widgets to see today's steps and your recent heatmap without opening the app.

SHARE YOUR PROGRESS
Export clean cards for today, this week, or the whole year and share them anywhere.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
Your health data never leaves your device. No account, no sign-up, no tracking.

Lace up and start filling your year. Every step counts.
