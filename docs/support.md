## Frequently asked questions

### The app shows 0 steps / no data
Stepy reads steps from Apple Health. Check that:

1. You granted access — **Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → Stepy →**
   turn on **Steps**.
2. Apple Health actually has step data. Open the **Health** app → **Browse →
   Activity → Steps**. If it's empty, your iPhone hasn't recorded steps yet.
3. You're on a real iPhone. Step data is generated by the device's motion
   sensors; the iOS Simulator has none.

### How do I change my daily goal?
Tap the goal area on the main screen and adjust it (steps go up/down in
increments). The new goal is saved automatically.

### How is the heatmap calculated?
Each square is one day. Brighter squares mean more steps relative to your goal.
Tap any square to see that day's exact step count.

### The widget isn't updating
Widgets refresh periodically rather than instantly. Open the app once to force a
refresh, and make sure the widget is added from the **Stepy** app. If it still
looks stale, remove and re-add the widget.

### Does Stepy track my location or send my data anywhere?
No. Stepy has no servers and no account. All your data stays on your device. See
the [Privacy Policy](privacy.md) for details.

### How do I revoke Health access?
**Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → Stepy**, then turn off the
permissions you want to revoke.

### I have a feature request or found a bug
